Max Denney / Yiseul LeMieux / Ben Rempel / Sarah Zahorodni

The Rivers We Live By

How do we collaborate effectively across distances? How do we create meaningful work in these “separate,” settings? How do we bring our environments and places into a collaboration when our co-conspirators can’t fully experience them?

In talking through ideas for this collaboration, we realized that we all live near rivers; the Charles, Hudson, Iowa, and Los Angeles Rivers, respectively. During a set window of time, we all walked down to our rivers to document them, through pictures, videos, field recordings, and text. Using these documents as our jumping off points, we each created a short piece, which we then passed in a circle, a la Exquisite Corpse, until we had 4 branching, layered works to which we all contributed. Yiseul then created videos for each, blending the images and film we had collected in our documentation with the final audio.
