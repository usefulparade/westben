Barbra Lica

⬊ info

What could be sunnier than a day on the Westben Meadow with Barbra Lica? Known from Japan to Manhattan, this internationally acclaimed Toronto-based singer-songwriter shares favourite pop-jazz chestnuts and brand new songs - some literally from the isolation of her basement! Joining Barbra are favourite bandmates Tom Fleming and Michael Eckert, along with up-and-coming songstress Sammy Jackson.

Artists: Barbra Lica, vocals; Tom Fleming, guitar; Michael Eckert on pedal steel/dobro; Sammy Jackson solo and backing vocals
