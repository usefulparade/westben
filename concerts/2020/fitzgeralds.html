Fitzgeralds

⬊ info

World champions at the Barn! Even the crickets and birds stop for a moment to watch in amazement. Dazzling footwork, fantastic fiddling and limitless energy pour out of these incredible musicians who share traditional and original Celtic-based music from Old Time fiddling and step-dancing to their own evocative arrangements.
