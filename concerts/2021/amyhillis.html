⬊ concert description

Sunshine, fresh air, and a solo violin! Violinist Amy Hillis performs amidst the splendour of the Westben Barn and meadow, as well as in the enchanting Mary West Reserve. There is no end to the enthusiastic curiosity of this exciting young Canadian violinist.

Loved by Westben audiences for her scintillating performance of Vivaldi’s The Four Seasons in 2019, Amy explores an array of music for solo violin written over the past 250 years including the music of Bach, Michael Osterle, Vincent Ho, Reena Esmail, Owen Zammit, and the Indigenous composer Wesley Hardisty.
