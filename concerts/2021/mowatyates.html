⬊ concert description

Join Alderville Chief Dave Mowat and guitarist Clayton Yates for Blues, wisdom & storytelling around The Campfire. Hear important stories about Mississauga and other First Nations people accompanied by the crackling of the fire.

⬊ artists

Dave Mowat

Alderville First Nations Chief, historian & lifelong musician. His style can best be described as Chicago, Delta, and country blues of a bygone era.

Clayton Yates

Expert at the guitar, mandolin, bass, and ukulele, Yates has been teaching music in Northumberland County since 2015. Yates has also performed and recorded professionally for the better part of two decades.
