⬊ concert description

Canada’s vibrant soprano & Brian Finley share favourites from Mozart & Puccini, ‘Till There was You’ (The Music Man) and Deliah’s “Song of Songs” from Brian’s musical SAMSON.

⬊ about the artist

Alberta‐born soprano Caitlin Wood received her Masters of Music in Opera Performance from University of Toronto. Caitlin was “absolutely vibrant” and showed “great comic flair” in her debut as Susanna in Vancouver Opera Festival’s The Marriage of Figaro. She gained attention as Joanna in Sweeney Todd while a member of Vancouver Opera’s Yulanda M. Faris Young Artist Program. Caitlin is a familiar face at Westben with years of previous performances at The Barn and we are thrilled to present a Digital Concert with her this year.
